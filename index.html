More Than Just Coffee

Welcome to Blue Tokai Coffee Roasters | Ballygunge. Located in the vibrant Garcha neighborhood, we bring the farm-to-cup experience directly to Kolkata.

Our beans are sourced directly from India's best coffee estates, roasted with care, and brewed to perfection by our expert baristas. Whether you need a quiet corner to work or a lively spot to catch up with friends, our ground and 1st-floor seating offers the perfect ambiance.
